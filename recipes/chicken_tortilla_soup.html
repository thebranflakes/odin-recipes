Chicken Tortilla Soup

Home

Ingredients

  • Chicken stock
  • Rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • Canned red enchilada sauce
  • Canned tomatoes. We used diced, fire-roasted.
  • Canned creamed or sweet corn

Directions

  1. Dump all of the ingredients shredded chicken, corn, enchilada sauce, tomatoes and stock into a big pot.
  2. Bring the soup to a boil, then simmer for about 15 minutes so the flavors meld, and the chicken gets infused with the wonderful Mexican spices in the enchilada sauce.
  3. Serve! Ladle into bowls and dig in.
